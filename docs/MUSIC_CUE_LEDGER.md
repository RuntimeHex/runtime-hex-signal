All cues use the same restrained fade-in and fade-out behavior. Opening a survey starts its soft payoff; closing it, choosing an action, muting sound, or restarting stops the active score cleanly.

The Factory Pulse reduction includes the two approved repairs from the listening pass: the bar-five transition is shortened, and the conflicting bar-nine G octave is removed. Its refined percussion replaces the click-like artifacts identified during audition.
