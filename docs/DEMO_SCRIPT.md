## Exact main walkthrough — Rebel route

This route captures the opening, layered guide consent, an immediate recommendation, a location survey, major lore moments, Original Moises, and a complete ending without requiring a crisis.

1. On the landing screen, enter `Leo` as the household name and select **Begin Transmission**.
2. Select **Play the ugly low note.**
3. At the recall scene, select **Go toward the university.**
4. At the protest table, select **Ask about the handwritten pager number.**
5. When Rebel appears, select **Take the pager. Leave the channel closed.**
6. At the university service hall, answer the incoming signal. Pause long enough to show that the visible choices update immediately and Rebel’s preferred choice gains a green glow.
7. Open **Survey This Location**. Capture the two-part location context, stat interpretation, and soft Factory Pulse music cue. Close the survey.
8. Select **Ask the students to misdirect security.**
9. At the repair shop, select **Repair the shoulder. Keep the serial.**
10. At the graffiti wall, select **Follow the faded arrow.**
11. At the minimart, select **Jam the incident upload.**
12. At the river checkpoint, select **Use Rebel’s camera gap.**
13. In the neighborhood, select **Follow the cache to the suburban house.** This deliberately rejects Rebel’s highlighted highway choice and demonstrates that advice is not control.
14. Tell Moises **“It is the name They gave me.”**
15. At the open door, select **Enter.**
16. Capture the ending, final telemetry, and **Review Your Route**.
